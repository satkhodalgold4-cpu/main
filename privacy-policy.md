# Privacy Policy – Sat Khodal Gold App

**Last updated:** 28 January 2026

Sat Khodal Gold App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparency in how data is handled. This Privacy Policy explains how the app interacts with Google services and how information is used.

---

## 1. Purpose of the App

Sat Khodal Gold App is designed to upload files to Google Drive for application-specific functionality.  
The app does **not** provide Google Sign-In or user authentication features.

---

## 2. Use of Google Drive API

Sat Khodal Gold App uses the **Google Drive API** exclusively for the following purpose:

- Uploading files to Google Drive

The app requests only the minimum required Google Drive access scope to perform this operation.

The app:
- Does **not** read existing files from Google Drive
- Does **not** delete or modify files outside of those it uploads
- Does **not** access Google account profile information

---

## 3. Google OAuth and Tokens

- Google OAuth is used only to authorize access to Google Drive
- A refresh token is securely stored to generate short-lived access tokens
- Tokens are used **only** to upload files to Google Drive
- Tokens are never shared with third parties or exposed publicly

No Google login is required within the app.

---

## 4. Information Collection

Sat Khodal Gold App does **not** collect, store, or process:

- Google account profile data
- Email addresses
- Contacts
- Personal user information

Only files explicitly uploaded by the app are transmitted to Google Drive.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- The app does not store Google Drive file contents outside of the upload process
- OAuth tokens are stored securely using industry-standard security practices
- Access to tokens is restricted and protected against unauthorized use

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, rent, or disclose any data to third parties.

Data is transmitted only between the app and Google Drive as required for file uploads.

---

## 7. Data Retention

- The app does not retain Google user data
- Uploaded files remain in Google Drive under the control of the Google account owner
- Users can revoke app access at any time via their Google Account settings

Access revocation link:
https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Sat Khodal Gold App is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
